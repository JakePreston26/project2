History of Prisons
Prisons have been around for a very long time, ever since there were laws there were people who broke them, and thus places or ways to deal with these people. Prisons have changed drastically over the years, from ancient midieval toture chambers to the prisons we have today. Prisons in America have also changes drastically over the years. One example is solitary confinement. It was taken away at one point because of its barbaric nature but it is back now.
Future of Prisons
Prisons are going to be around forever. As long as there are laws, there will be people who break them. Prisons should not be for punishing people though, they should be for rehabilitating people into society and helping make sure they have the tools and willpower to not commit any more crimes in the future. Our prison system right now is about punishment. It is about getting rid of the people society does not like, and that is a bad system. Prisons in the future must help prisoners and others, not hurt then.
